About PViz:

PViz is a visualization tool that allows the graphical representation of the peer network and the execution process of a PeerSim simulation.
It supports many useful operations such as zooming-in on a specific area of a network, node monitoring/highlighting, single or multi-step re-execution of the simulation and appropriate visual represenation of an overlay network.>


Implementation:

Currently it supports the fundamental structured protocol of Chord and P-Grid. More protocols and a more abstract implementation is currently in the works.
